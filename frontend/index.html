ERAU AIDB LHUFT Database

The Language as a Human Factor Accident Investigation Database compiles information related to the role of language in accident and serious incident investigations.

(Althea)
Mission Statement/Purpose

For our internal use:
The PURPOSE of the LHUFT AIDB is to compile information on possible language-related factors so that

a) researchers, applied linguists, human factors specialists, aviation operational specialists, and accident investigators can develop an understanding of the various ways that language affects aviation safety, and

b) Information related to language in accident and serious incident investigation reports is easily accessible to the aviation safety community.
